Enum ConsignmentAddressType

Defines the purpose of the Consignment Contact Address

Namespace: Sorted.PRO.SDK.DataTypes.Consignments.Enums
Assembly: Sorted.PRO.SDK.DataTypes.Consignments.dll
Syntax
[DataContract]
[Flags]

public enum ConsignmentAddressType

Fields

Name Description
Destination

Destination contact address. This is the final desintation of the consignment as far as the carrier is concerned.
Importer

This is the party liable for the payment of duties on the consignment. In the case of a multi-leg shipment, this could be the carrier.
Origin

Origin contact address. This is the ORIGIN of the consignment as far as the carrier is concerned
Recipient

The receiver of the consignment E.g. For a pick up, this could be the party who picks up the consingment from it's 'Destination' address
Return

The return address of the contact address. This is where the consignment will/could be sent if say the consignment could not be delivered and the carrier supports a return address.
Sender

The contact address of the sender. E.g. For a drop off, this could be the contact address of the party dropping off the consignment at the 'Origin' address
